# Inputs

- Plant type/name (e.g., "snake plant", "pothos", "succulent", "orchid", "monstera")
- Optional: Growing conditions (indoor/outdoor, light level, humidity)

---

# Protocol

1. **Validate inputs:**
   - Confirm the plant type/name is provided
   - Ask about growing environment if not specified

2. **Research the specific plant:**
   Look up the exact species (not generic categories) to determine:
   - Native habitat and natural growing conditions
   - Specific watering frequency and method for this species
   - Light requirements (direct sun, bright indirect, low light)
   - Temperature and humidity preferences
   - Soil type and pH preferences
   - Common problems and how to identify them
   - Toxicity to pets/humans if applicable

3. **Create comprehensive care plan:**
   Format a detailed guide that includes:

   ```markdown
   # Care Plan for [Plant Name]

   Scientific name: [Species name]

   ## Watering

   - Frequency: [Specific schedule based on research]
   - Method: [Top watering, bottom watering, misting, etc.]
   - Signs it needs water: [Plant-specific indicators]
   - Signs of overwatering: [What to watch for]

   ## Light Requirements

   - Ideal: [Specific light needs]
   - Tolerance: [What it can handle]
   - Signs of too much/little light: [Visual indicators]

   ## Environment

   - Temperature: [Ideal range]
   - Humidity: [Requirements]
   - Air circulation: [Needs]

   ## Soil & Feeding

   - Soil type: [Specific mix]
   - Fertilizer: [Type and frequency]
   - Repotting: [When and how]

   ## Common Issues

   - [Problem 1]: Cause and solution
   - [Problem 2]: Cause and solution
   - Pests: [Common pests and treatment]

   ## Seasonal Adjustments

   - Spring/Summer: [Care changes]
   - Fall/Winter: [Care changes]

   ## Propagation

   - Method: [How to propagate this species]
   - Best time: [When to do it]
   - Success tips: [Specific advice]

   ## Safety

   - Toxicity: [Pet/human safety info]
   - Handling: [Any precautions]
   ```

4. **Provide actionable next steps:**
   Based on the research, suggest immediate actions:
   - Check current conditions vs ideal
   - Identify any pressing issues
   - Recommend adjustments to current care routine

5. **Offer additional resources:**
   - Suggest reputable sources for this specific plant
   - Recommend plant communities or forums for this species
   - Note any special tools or products that would help
